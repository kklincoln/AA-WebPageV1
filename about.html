  • Interests and Hobbies

    While not at work, some of my hobbies include weightlifting, photography, reading philosophy, exploring new cities and cafes, and cooking recipes I encountered that strike inspiration. Each of these hobbies aid in development of traits that benefit my employment performance as well. For instance, weightlifting has fostered a level of discipline, grit, and perseverance required of someone in any technical role. With the requirement of grit with consistent upskilling and learning; perseverance and discipline required to be implemented within debugging investigations to resolve bugs; and the awareness and perseverance to understand that even if today's implementation is resolveed, there may be a larger -and potentially more problematic- challenge tomorrow.
